(unofficial)
CA Wii Bowling Tournament
2026

Play Wii Bowling in a group of four people,
but don't compete against each other...
Compete against the world!

How does this work?

This is an asynchronous tournament that you can participate in any time the red Wii is available before the end of the tournament.
Participating is simple! All you have to do is:

  1. Assemble a team of four CA's!
  2. Play a standard game of bowling in Wii Sports Resort!
  3. Take a picture of the score screen when the game is over!
  4. Submit the online form! It requires a file upload, so you'll have to be logged into Google for it to work.

When does it end?

I'll announce the winners at 5:00 PM on Friday, March 13, 2026!

What are the rules?

  • All participants must be a CA (current or veteran).
  • You need a full team of four people, and each person needs to participate! One person can't play as multiple users in a single game. If someone has to leave early, it's okay to sub in someone else. You'll just need to figure out whose name to put in the entry form.
  • The photo you upload in the form needs to contain the scores of all four participants directly from the game. I can't verify the game without them!
  • Only bowling games in Wii Sports Resort count towards the tournament (not Wii Sports).
  • Teams are allowed to submit entries for multiple games! But if there is a team that hasn't played yet and wants a turn, be sure to give them a chance to play :)
  • One person can't play for more than one team. Or two teams. I don't know. This is kind of a loose rule. Just don't submit as a member of too many teams!

Are there prizes?

I made some badges that took me a whole thirty minutes to design, so you'll definitely be getting those!

What happens in the event of a tie?

Honestly, I don't know! Maybe I'll flip a coin and pick a winner at random? If some members of the tie-breaking teams are around by the end of the week to do an extra game, maybe they can do an "overtime" game? I'll figure it out later... probably.
